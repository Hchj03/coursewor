Ratchet

Ratchet is a Lombax: a race that seems to have disappeared from the universe a long time ago. While Ratchet has always had a primary goal of saving the galaxy, finding his roots was in the back of his mind, most prominently in Ratchet and Clank Future: Tools of Destruction. On his home planet of Veldin, the Lombax takes up building a spaceship as a hobby. Later on in the series, we discover that Ratchet is an extremely skilled mechanic as he repairs and synthesizes most of his things in mere seconds by using his trusty wrench: the OmniWrench 8000. The wrench is not only a tool, however, but also a weapon. Ratchet's most basic melee ability is to swing and slam the wrench, destroying some of the enemies in one strike. Clank usually rides his back, allowing Ratchet to use him for help throughout his adventures.


Clank

Clank is a small robot, programmed with an artificial intelligence, and he is also Ratchet's long-time companion and friend. He was created at a robot plant on the planet Quartu. The name "Clank" emerged when Ratchet made his handmade ship rock while flying to planet Novalis, causing the small robot to hit the side of the ship and making a clank sound. Clank's serial number was B5429671.
